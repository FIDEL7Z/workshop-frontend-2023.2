Star Wars

"Star Wars" is an iconic science fiction franchise created by George Lucas. Initiated in 1977 with the movie "Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope," the saga transports viewers to a distant galaxy, immersing them in epic conflicts between the Jedi and the Sith.

Through its films, TV series, books and comics, "Star Wars" explores themes of heroism, adventure and the Force, captivating fans of all ages. Its memorable characters such as Luke Skywalker, Princess Leia, Darth Vader and Yoda, along with their thrilling space battles, have become pop culture icons.
